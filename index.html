FAQ

Questions founders ask us

How is this different from hiring a lead gen agency on retainer?

A retainer agency gets paid the same whether your calendar fills up or not — their incentive is to keep the contract alive. We're priced per appointment, not per month: every appointment is qualified against criteria we agree on upfront, and if one doesn't meet the bar or doesn't show, we replace it. That single difference changes everything about how the work gets done.

What counts as a "qualified" appointment?

Whatever we agree it means for your business — before launch, in writing. Typically that covers industry, company size, budget authority, and that the person on the call is a decision-maker. If a booking doesn't meet the criteria, or doesn't show up, we replace it.

How does pricing work exactly?

Priced per appointment — we'll walk you through the exact numbers and how onboarding works on the qualification call, because the right structure depends on your deal size and volume targets. What we can tell you now: there's no bloated monthly retainer, and every appointment is priced against the criteria we agree on upfront.

How fast will I see appointments?

We'll give you an honest timeline on the call based on your market and offer. What we won't do is hide behind a six-month "ramp-up period" — our model doesn't reward us for stalling.

What do you need from me?

About an hour during onboarding to lock in your qualification criteria and positioning, and a sales team (or you) ready to take the calls. We handle everything up to the booked meeting.

Why do you make people book a "qualification call" first?

Because we get paid per result, we can't afford to take on offers we can't fill calendars for. The call is us qualifying you as much as you qualifying us — that's exactly the standard we then apply to every prospect we book for you.
